Upon starting my PhD, one of the first researchers I was introduced to was [Uri Alon](https://www.weizmann.ac.il/mcb/alon/group) and his work on systems biology. I was immediately struck by his ideas of combining simple yet effective mathematical models.

These posts will be a walkthrough of the ideas discussed in his book [An Introduction to Systems Biology](https://doi.org/10.1201/9780429283321). As I am not a biologist, I'll try my level best to explain the ideas as I understand, and build the intuition from the ground up.
