Hi, I am Prasoon!

I am currently finishing up my master’s in computational astrophysics at the Friedrich-Schiller-Universität Jena and working as a ML student researcher in Dr. Dönertas group at Fritz Lipmann Institute.

My research interests lie at the interface of machine learning, dynamical systems modeling, and evolutionary theory of aging, with a focus on developing biologically and mechanistically grounded frameworks for understanding aging.

In parallel to my work on aging, I am developing mlgw_bns_HOM, a neural network-driven frequency-domain waveform generator for binary neutron star mergers.
