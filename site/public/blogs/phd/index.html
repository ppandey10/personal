  • 01/01/0001 Regulatory Capacity Project

    Abstract for ISCB 2026 Background: Aging is increasingly described as the progressive loss of regulatory control, a decline strongly reflected in DNA methylation (DNAm). At each CpG site, the methylation state is actively maintained by a balance between writing DNMTs and erasing TETs; as this balance falters with age, the methylome drifts toward global hypomethylation with focal hypermethylation. The way this breakdown plays out at individual sites, however, remains poorly quantified.
