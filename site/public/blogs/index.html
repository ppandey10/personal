Blogs

Just a place to think out loud about systems biology, AI, and aging, from the perspective of a physicist learning the field. BIOLOGY IS COMPLEX in ways physics rarely is, and that raises a question I keep coming back to: can we model it? If yes, how well? And where do those models quietly break down? These posts are my attempt to work through that, documenting the ideas and limitations that define quantitative biology today.
